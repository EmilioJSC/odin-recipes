Bacon Ranch Cheese Ball



Description:
This is an extra bacon-y version of my go-to ranch cheese ball recipe. If you're a bacon or ranch lover (or both),
this is the cheese ball dreams are made of, and will be a staple appetizer for entertaining.

Prep Time: Cook Time: Chill Time: Total Time: Servings: Yield:
15 mins 10 mins 2 hrs 2 hrs 25 mins 32 1 Large cheese ball

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix
  • 1 teaspoon dried minced onion
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ cup minced fresh chives
  • crackers for serving

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat,
    turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
    Drain bacon slices on paper towels and allow to cool.
    Crumble bacon slices when cool enough to handle.

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix together cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, Ranch dressing mix,
    dried minced onion, garlic powder, and 1/2 of the crumbled bacon
    in a bowl until well combined. Place the other half of crumbled
    bacon in the refrigerator until ready to use.

  3. Step 3

    4. Form cheese mixture into a round ball (it's helpful to use
    plastic wrap while shaping it). Wrap in plastic wrap and
    refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place remaining crumbled bacon on a plate and stir in minced
    chives. Remove plastic wrap from the cheese ball and roll it
    in the bacon and chive mixture, lightly pressing the topping
    to the cheese ball so it sticks.

  5. Step 5

    6. Place the coated cheese ball on a serving dish and surround
    with crackers for dipping.
